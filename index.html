The most famous desserts around the world

Crème brûlée

known as burnt cream or Trinity cream, and virtually identical to crema catalana, is a dessert consisting of a rich custard base topped with a layer of hardened caramelized sugar.

Tiramisu

of ladyfinger pastries dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone and flavoured with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

Black Forest Cake

A traditional German dessert that makes for a delightful centerpiece at any dinner party is their decadent black forest cake. It’s fruity, creamy, chocolatey and has a good dash of liquor in there too – what’s not to love about that?.

Churros

Kids and grownups both delight in these deep-fried pastry sticks from Spain. The piped pastry is dipped into hot oil until crisp and golden, and then coated in sugar, chocolate or dulce de leche. Best eaten on a brisk day from a local street vendor..

Baklava

This gorgeous dessert comes in many different varieties. The base is made from layers of flaky pastry soaked in sweet syrup and butter and flavored with orange blossom water. Baklava is stuffed with chopped nuts and can come in many flavors such as cardamom, rose, and pistachio..

Chocolate brownie

brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust.
